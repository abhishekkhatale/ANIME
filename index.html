LUFI

Luffy, also known as Monkey D. Luffy, is the main protagonist of the popular anime and manga series "One Piece." He is a determined and adventurous young pirate with the dream of becoming the Pirate King. Luffy possesses the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit, granting him rubber-like abilities, and leads the Straw Hat Pirates on their journey to find the legendary treasure known as One Piece.

ZORO

Roronoa Zoro, commonly known as Zoro, is a prominent character in the anime and manga series "One Piece." He is a highly skilled swordsman and the first member to join Luffy's crew, the Straw Hat Pirates. Zoro's dream is to become the world's greatest swordsman, and he wields a unique three-sword style in combat. He is known for his unwavering loyalty, strong sense of justice, and determination to surpass his limits.

ACE

Portgas D. Ace, commonly referred to as Ace, is a significant character in the anime and manga series "One Piece." He is the older brother of Monkey D. Luffy and the son of the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. Ace is a powerful and skilled pirate who possesses the power of the Mera Mera no Mi, a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows him to control and create fire.
